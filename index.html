Adventure Sark
ActivityCoasteering · Kayaking · Team events
+
Local company with local guides. Coasteering, kayaking, Activiquiz, Corporate Fun Days and Team Building. Available for Hen & Stag parties, birthdays, schools, Scouts & Guides and corporate groups.
📞 +44 7781 130 403EmailWebsite ↗

Sark Boat Trips
Water2.5 hr island tour · 11am & 2pm
£30pp
+
Departing from Creux Harbour on the Dorado. 2.5 hr trip around the island exploring bays, coves and secret caves. Spot puffins, guillemots, razorbills, cormorants, dolphins, seals and even basking sharks. Knowledgeable guide Morgan.
⏰ Usually 11:00 & 14:00 daily (weather dependent) · Pay on the day by card or cash
Book online ↗
🫏
Sark Donkey Walks
WildlifeWalk with Florence & Ruby · Family friendly
+
Meet Florence and Ruby, Sark's resident donkeys, and take them for a gentle guided walk to see some of the loveliest coastal views on the island. Walks can be short or long and can include a picnic lunch stop on request. Weather dependent — donkeys don't like the rain!
🐾 Great for families · Picnic lunch stop available on request · Weather dependent
🌿
La Seigneurie Gardens
AttractionWalled gardens · Heritage rooms · Maze
+
Set between flower-strewn granite walls and the towers of one of Sark's most historic buildings. The walled garden overflows with fascinating flowers and shrubs. Includes a Heritage Room with free entry (Mon–Fri, Easter to end of September), a maze, and the Seigneurs' chapel.
🕐 Heritage Room: Mon–Fri, Easter–end September, free entry
Website ↗
🍫
Caragh Chocolates
AttractionChocolate kitchen · Café · Workshop
+
Visit the chocolate kitchen and watch handmade chocolates being created using Sark's famous Channel Island cream. Enjoy the café with panoramic sea views of all the islands, and book a workshop to make your own chocolates. Also has a small outdoor pool available for a small fee.
🪨
Sark Henge & Militia Trail
HeritageSelf-guided · QR code trail
+
Follow the Militia Trail using a leaflet from the Visitor Centre and discover Sark's defence fortifications. The QR code trail features small plaques at points of historical interest. Sark Henge is a set of small standing stones on a headland — the holes line up with other island landmarks.
📍 Leaflets from the Sark Visitor Centre · Self-guided · Free
🌟
Dark Sky Stargazing
AttractionSark Observatory · No light pollution
+
Sark is a designated Dark Sky Island — one of the best places in the British Isles for stargazing. With no cars or street lights, the Milky Way is visible on clear nights. Visit the Sark Observatory for events throughout the season.
